Web Designer

Web designers plan, design, and code websites and web pages, many of which include audio, video, graphics, and other media. The design and layout of a website or online pages are the responsibility of a web designer. It might also imply creating a fresh new website or modifying an existing one.

Animator

An animator's job is to create a series of images called frames that resemble movement. Working with creative experts such as storytellers, designers, and voice actors to develop films, video games, or graphics for advertisements and other kinds of television entertainment is one of their responsibilities.

Video Editor

A Video Editor is responsible for transforming raw material shot by a film team and director into the final output. This entails following a plan, script, or shot list and putting the footage together into a single movie or film. Many camera angles and takes are frequently recorded in video and film work.

Social Media Manager

A Social Media Manager, also known as a Community Manager, is in charge of a company's public relations through implementing content strategy on social media platforms. Analyzing engagement data, recognizing trends in customer interactions, and developing digital initiatives to build community online are among their responsibilities.

Graphic Designer

A Graphic Designer, often known as a Graphic Artist, is in charge of generating visually appealing pictures to accompany written information. Meeting with clients to determine their needs, completing projects using design tools, and updating projects based on client input are among their responsibilities. Creates general design and layout for commercials, brochures, and business reports, among other things.

Digital Marketing Specialist

A Digital Marketing Specialist, also known as a Digital Marketer, is responsible for assisting in the maintenance of a brand through marketing efforts. Market research, brainstorming with other marketing experts, and developing material to aid in the success of marketing initiatives are among their responsibilities.
